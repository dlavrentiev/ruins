Kharkiv National University of Urban Economy

Location: Kharkiv, Kharkiv Oblast
Type: Educational | Status: Partially Destroyed

Kharkiv National University of Urban Economy is a Ukrainian university in Kharkiv, named after Oleksii Beketov (Ukr. Олексій Бекетов) and specializing in urban development studies.

During the Russian invasion of Ukraine on June 20, 2022, the housing and communal college of the university was destroyed by a rocket attack. On July 23, a shell from a cannon exploded in the courtyard of the main building, knocking out windows and damaging the monument to Oleksii Beketov. As of January 2023, 16 out of 21 buildings and all 10 university dormitories were damaged by Russian strikes. The latest strike on February 5, 2023, partially destroyed the main building and damaged the administrative, architectural and museum buildings.
