Maternity Hospital No. 3

Location: Mariupol, Donetsk Oblast
Type: Health Care | Status: Destroyed

On 9 March 2022, the Russian Air Force bombed Maternity Hospital No. 3 (Ukr. Пологовий будинок № 3), a hospital complex functioning both as a children's hospital and maternity ward in during the Russian invasion of Ukraine, killing at least four people and injuring at least sixteen, and leading to at least one stillbirth.
